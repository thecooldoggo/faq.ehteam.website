Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How can I play in customs?

To join NickEh30's custom games, you need to be a paid subscriber on his Twitch channel. Watch the tutorial video below for a guide. A text explanation is also in the video description.

What is NickEh30's Discord?

Join NickEh30's official Discord server by clicking here.

Why does Nick use Yunite?

Nick uses Yunite to prevent griefers from disrupting games by hiding the custom matchmaking code.

How can I talk in NickEh30's Discord?

To chat in the EhTeam Discord server, you need to be a paid subscriber on Twitch, YouTube, or YouTube Shorts. Link your Discord account to your Twitch or YouTube account in the "Connections" section of Discord settings.

When will NickEh30 go live?

Nick's live stream schedule can change. Stay updated by watching the #updates channel in the Discord server!

Can we play customs?

NickEh30 decides when to host custom games. Please don't repeatedly ask for customs to avoid a timeout, and wait for announcements.

How can I request an unban?

If you think you were unfairly banned, send an unban request to nickeh30ehteam@gmail.com with your username and details of the situation.

Is Trusted still used?

The Trusted system is no longer accepting applications. NickEh30 and the moderation team use other methods to manage griefers.

Other Questions?

If you have more questions, ask in the #help channel in the EhTeam Discord server or reach out to a trusted community member.
