Why does Nick use Yunite?

Nick uses Yunite to help prevent griefers, as it hides the custom matchmaking code.

How can I talk in NickEh30's Discord?

To chat in the EhTeam, you'll need to be a paid subscriber on one of NickEh30's channels: Twitch, YouTube, YouTube Shorts. Once you're subscribed, link your Discord account to your Twitch or YouTube account to unlock chat access in the EhTeam. To link your twitch and discord account, first open settings, go to "Connections" click on "Twitch" and follow the prompts.

When will NickEh30 go live?

We don't know when Nick will go live! Keep an eye on the #updates channel in the discord... 👀

Can we play customs?

NickEh30 will do customs whenever he feels ready! Please do not ask for them repeatedly, doing so for a can lead to a timeout!

How can I request an unban?

To request an unban, send an email to nickeh30ehteam@gmail.com. Please include your username, explanation, and anything else that would be helpful.

Is Trusted still used?

Nick is not reading applications for the Trusted system anymore, so don’t worry about submitting an application.

Other Questions?
